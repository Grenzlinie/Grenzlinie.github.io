Computational Materials Researcher

Siyu Liu

I am a Ph.D. candidate in the Materials Theory Group at HKU, building autonomous workflows that connect atomistic simulations, scientific foundation models, and large language models for accelerated discovery. My recent collaborations span multi-agent reasoning for materials laws, elastic tensor prediction, and generative design for inverse problems.
